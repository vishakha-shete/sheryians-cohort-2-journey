# 📖 What is Off-Campus Placement?

Off-campus placement refers to applying for jobs outside the college placement cell. Students directly apply to companies through job portals, LinkedIn, referrals, or company career pages.

Unlike on-campus hiring, off-campus placements are open to all eligible candidates and happen throughout the year.

---

# ⚖️ On-Campus vs Off-Campus Placement

| Feature             | On-Campus             | Off-Campus            |
| ------------------- | --------------------- | --------------------- |
| Hiring Process      | Through College       | Direct Company Hiring |
| Eligibility         | College Students Only | Open to Everyone      |
| Flexibility         | Limited               | High                  |
| Number of Companies | Limited               | Unlimited             |
| Hiring Period       | Fixed                 | Year-round            |
