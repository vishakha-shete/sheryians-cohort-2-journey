# 🚀 Sheryians Cohort 2.0 – Final Wrap-Up Session

  * System Design

* Tailor your resume for every job.
* Stay consistent on:

  * GitHub
  * LinkedIn
  * LeetCode
* Your journey starts now—the cohort is just the foundation. 

---

### 🧠 Anas Ansari

* DSA is still one of the most important skills for top companies.
* AI can generate code, but it cannot replace strong problem-solving ability.
* Learn concepts first, then solve problems repeatedly.
* Follow spaced repetition:

  * Solve today
  * Repeat after 5 days
  * Repeat after 10 days
* Maintain an Excel sheet to track patterns and revision.
* Focus on clarity instead of solving hundreds of questions.
* Use ChatGPT for hints and algorithms—not direct solutions.
