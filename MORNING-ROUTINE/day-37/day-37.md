## 🚀 Key Learning

I don't need perfect English.

I need:

* Confidence
* Clear communication
* Honesty
* Willingness to learn

The interviewer is not expecting perfection from a fresher.

They want to understand:

* Who I am
* What I know
* How I think
* Whether I am willing to learn and grow
