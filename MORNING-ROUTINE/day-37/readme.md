Q4. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by websites in a user's browser.

They help websites remember user information such as login sessions, preferences, language settings, and browsing activity.

Q5. Types of Cookies
Essential Cookies

Website cannot function without them.

Examples:

Login session
Shopping cart
Functional Cookies

Remember preferences.

Examples:

Language
Theme
Analytics Cookies

Track usage.

Examples:

Google Analytics
Advertising Cookies

Used for marketing.

Examples:

Facebook Pixel
Google Ads
Q6. What is a Cookie Consent Banner?

A cookie consent banner is a notice shown to users requesting permission to store cookies and collect data.

Users can accept, reject, or customize their cookie preferences.

Q7. Why are Cookie Banners Important?

They help websites comply with privacy regulations and ensure users have control over how their data is collected and used.

Q8. First-Party vs Third-Party Cookies
First-Party

Created by website itself.

Example:

amazon.com

stores login information.

Third-Party

Created by external services.

Example:

Google Analytics
Facebook Pixel
